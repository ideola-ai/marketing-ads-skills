# Content Pillars Reference

Template untuk menentukan content pillars saat menjalankan `/content-plan`.

## 5 Common Content Pillars

### 1. Educational
Teach your audience something valuable.
- **Examples**: How-to guides, tips, tutorials, industry insights
- **Format**: Carousels, infographics, long-form captions
- **Goal**: Build authority and trust

### 2. Entertaining
Create content that delights and engages.
- **Examples**: Humor, trends, behind-the-scenes, relatable moments
- **Format**: Reels, stories, short videos
- **Goal**: Increase reach and engagement

### 3. Inspirational
Motivate and inspire your audience.
- **Examples**: Success stories, quotes, transformations, vision
- **Format**: Graphics, reels, testimonials
- **Goal**: Emotional connection

### 4. Promotional
Showcase your products or services.
- **Examples**: Product features, offers, launches, demos
- **Format**: Product photos, demos, testimonials
- **Goal**: Drive conversions

### 5. Engagement
Encourage interaction and build community.
- **Examples**: Questions, polls, UGC, discussions
- **Format**: Stories with stickers, post questions, threads
- **Goal**: Build community

## Content Mix Formula (The 4-1-1 Rule)

For every 6 posts:
- **4 Educational/Entertaining** (value-first content)
- **1 Soft Promotion** (subtle product mention)
- **1 Hard Promotion** (direct sales pitch)

## Example Content Pillars for Different Brands

### E-commerce Brand
1. Product Education (how to use)
2. User Results (testimonials)
3. Behind the Scenes (manufacturing)
4. Styling Tips (outfit ideas)
5. Promotions (sales, launches)

5. Service Promotions

### Personal Brand
1. Expertise Sharing
2. Personal Stories
3. Behind the Scenes
4. Curated Resources
