# Hooks Reference - Social Media & Ads

Daftar hook patterns yang effective untuk social media dan iklan.

## Curiosity Hooks

### Pattern 1: The Secret/Fact
- "Here's what nobody tells you about [topic]..."
- "The secret to [result] that [experts] won't share..."
- "[Number] things I wish I knew before [event]..."

### Pattern 2: The Mistake
- "Stop making this [topic] mistake..."
- "Why your [thing] isn't working..."
- "The #1 reason people fail at [goal]..."

### Pattern 3: The Counter-Intuitive
- "Why you should NOT [common advice]..."
- "Doing [opposite of common wisdom] changed my [result]..."
- "Unpopular opinion: [controversial statement]..."

## Value Hooks

### Pattern 4: The Promise
- "How I got [result] in [timeframe]..."
- "The exact [framework/system] I used to [result]..."
- "[Number] ways to get [result] without [pain point]..."

### Pattern 5: The List
- "[Number] [things] that actually work..."
- "My top [number] [resources/tools] for [goal]..."
- "Only [number] [things] you need for [result]..."

### Pattern 6: The Story
- "I almost gave up on [goal] until..."
- "How I went from [bad situation] to [good result]..."
- "The day everything changed for my [business/life]..."

## Social Proof Hooks

### Pattern 7: The Result
- "Client went from [starting point] to [result]..."
- "This [strategy/tactic] generated [number] in [timeframe]..."
- "Don't take my word for it, here's what happened..."

### Pattern 8: The Authority
- "After [number] years in [industry], here's what I learned..."
- "I've analyzed [number] [things], and here's the pattern..."
- "Having worked with [number] [clients/people], one thing stands out..."

## Platform-Specific Notes

### TikTok/Reels (Hook in first 1-3 seconds)
- Visual must match the hook
- Use trending sounds strategically
- Text overlays for accessibility

### Instagram (Hook in first frame/line)
- Carousel covers need visual hook
- First line of caption must stop the scroll
- Save-worthy content performs best

### LinkedIn (Hook in first 2 lines)
- Professional but conversational
- Data and stories work well
- Formatting matters (use line breaks)

### Twitter/X (Hook is the tweet itself)
- Thread starters need strong hook
- Use numbers and specific details
- Leave room for engagement

## Hook Testing Checklist

- [ ] Does it stop the scroll?
- [ ] Is it specific (not generic)?
- [ ] Does it deliver on the promise?
- [ ] Is it relevant to the audience?
- [ ] Does it match the content that follows?
