# Instagram Content Guidelines

## Platform Overview
- **Primary formats:** Feed posts, Stories, Reels
- **Aspect ratios:** 1:1, 4:5, 9:16
- **Best times:** 9-11 AM, 7-9 PM local time
- **Vibe:** Aesthetic, curated, visual-first

## Feed Posts

### Static Posts
- **Dimensions:** 1080x1080 (square) or 1080x1350 (4:5)
- **Caption length:** 150-300 characters optimal (first 2 lines visible)
- **Hashtags:** 15-30 relevant hashtags
- **Posting:** 3-5 per week maximum

### Carousel Posts
- **Dimensions:** Same as static, per slide
- **Slide count:** 5-10 slides optimal
- **First slide:** Critical hook - make it count
- **Last slide:** Always include CTA

### Caption Structure
```
[Hook - first 1-2 lines, this is all people see initially]

[Expanded value - 2-4 sentences]

[CTA - clear action]

[Hashtags - all at bottom or first comment]
```

## Stories

### Format
- **Dimensions:** 1080x1920 (9:16)
- **Duration:** 15 seconds max per story
- **Daily posting:** 3-5 stories optimal
- **Retention:** 50%+ viewership is good

### Story Flow
1. **Hook story** - Poll, question, or tease
2. **Content stories** - 2-4 stories with value
3. **CTA story** - Clear next step

### Interactive Elements
- **Poll:** Quick engagement, get opinions
- **Quiz:** Educational content
- **Slider:** Measure sentiment
- **Question:** Collect responses
- **Countdown:** Build anticipation
- **Link:** (10k+ followers) Add link sticker

### Story Highlights
- Organize by topic/theme
- Create branded covers
- Keep evergreen content accessible

## Reels

### Format
- **Dimensions:** 1080x1920 (9:16)
- **Length:** 15-60 seconds optimal
- **Hook:** 0-2 seconds critical
- **Posting:** 2-3 per week for growth

### Reel Structure
```
0-2s: Hook (visual or audio)
2-45s: Main content (value)
45-60s: CTA (follow, link, save)
```

### Reel Best Practices
- Use trending sounds (check Instagram Trends)
- Fast cuts every 1-3 seconds
- Text overlays for accessibility
- Cover image: select best frame
- Caption: Keep brief, key points only

## Hashtag Strategy

### Mix (for 20-30 hashtags)
- **5 super broad:** #business #marketing #growth
- **10 niche:** #contentstrategy #socialmediatips
- **10 specific:** #indonesiaentrepreneur #jakartabusiness
- **5 branded:** #yourbrand #yourcampaign

### Placement
- All in caption (below CTA)
- Or in first comment (cleaner look)

## Visual Style for Instagram

### Feed Aesthetic
- Consistent color palette
- Similar editing style
- Coherent grid layout
- Brand elements visible

### Popular Aesthetics
- **Minimalist:** Clean, simple, lots of white space
- **Bold:** Bright colors, high contrast, eye-catching
- **Warm:** Tones of orange, yellow, soft lighting
- **Cool:** Blues, greens, moody lighting
- **Vintage:** Film look, grain, muted colors

## Instagram Algorithm Notes

### Feed Ranking Signals
- **Relationship:** Do they engage with you?
- **Interest:** Have they engaged with similar content?
- **Timeliness:** How recent is the post?
- **Frequency:** How often do they open Instagram?
- **Following:** How many accounts do they follow?
- **Usage:** How long do they spend on Instagram?

### What Boosts Reach
- Saves (strongest signal)
- Shares
- Comments (meaningful, not generic)
- Profile visits from your post
- Time spent viewing

### What Hurts Reach
- Low engagement on your post
- Users scrolling past quickly
- Shadowban risk: Using banned hashtags

## Common Mistakes

1. **Wall of text** - Break up with line breaks and emojis
2. **Weak hooks** - First line must grab attention
3. **Inconsistent posting** - Algorithm favors consistency
4. **Ignoring Stories** - Stories build relationships
5. **Buying followers** - Kills engagement rate
6. **Wrong hashtags** - Use relevant, properly sized tags
7. **Not engaging** - Reply to comments, engage in your niche
