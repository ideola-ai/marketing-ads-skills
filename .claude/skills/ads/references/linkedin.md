# LinkedIn Ads Audit Guide

## Ad Formats

### 1. Sponsored Content (Single Image)
**Best for:** Brand awareness, website traffic, lead gen
**Key Checks:**
- Engagement rate (likes, comments, shares)
- Click-through rate
- Cost per send
- Content format performance

**Benchmarks:**
- CTR: 0.4-0.8%
- CPC: $4-8
- Engagement rate: 0.5-1.5%

### 2. Sponsored Content (Carousel)
**Best for:** Storytelling, multiple features
**Key Checks:**
- Card-by-card completion rate
- CTR by card position
- Cost per acquisition

**Benchmarks:**
- CTR: 0.5-1.0%
- Completion rate: 40-60%
- CPC: $5-10

### 3. Sponsored Content (Video)
**Best for:** Brand storytelling, product demos
**Key Checks:**
- Video view rate (25%, 50%, 75%, 100%)
- Completion rate
- Engagement vs. views

**Benchmarks:**
- View rate (50%): 25-40%
- Completion rate: 30-50%
- CTR: 0.6-1.2%

### 4. Message Ads (Sponsored Messaging)
**Best for:** Direct outreach, lead nurturing
**Key Checks:**
- Send rate (delivery)
- Open rate
- Click rate
- Unsubscribe rate

**Benchmarks:**
- Send rate: 95%+
- Open rate: 30-50%
- Click rate: 10-20%
- Unsubscribe rate: <2%

### 5. Lead Gen Forms (Document Ads / Lead Gen)
**Best for:** High-intent lead collection
**Key Checks:**
- Form opens
- Form completion rate
- Cost per lead
- Lead quality score

**Benchmarks:**
- Open rate: 20-30%
- Completion rate: 15-25%
- Cost per lead: $20-100 (varies by industry)

### 6. Text Ads
**Best for:** Direct response, promotions
**Key Checks:**
- CTR
- CPC
- Conversion rate

**Benchmarks:**
- CTR: 0.2-0.4%
- CPC: $3-6
- Conversion rate: 2-4%

## LinkedIn-Specific Metrics

| Metric | Good | Needs Help |
|--------|------|------------|
| Engagement Rate | 1%+ | <0.3% |
| CTR (Sponsored) | 0.7%+ | <0.3% |
| Open Rate (Message) | 40%+ | <25% |
| Form Completion | 20%+ | <10% |
| Send Rate | 98%+ | <90% |
| Unsubscribe Rate | <1% | >3% |

## Targeting Best Practices

### Audience Size Ranges
| Targeting Type | Optimal Size |
|----------------|--------------|
| Job Titles | 50,000 - 200,000 |
| Industries | 100,000 - 500,000 |
| Skills | 75,000 - 300,000 |
| Companies | 50,000 - 250,000 |

**Warning:** Audiences < 10,000 may not deliver consistently

### Targeting Attributes to Test
- **Job Titles:** Exact vs. related titles
- **Job Functions:** Broad functional targeting
- **Seniority:** Individual contributor vs. manager vs. VP/C-suite
- **Industries:** Vertical-specific messaging
- **Company Size:** Employee count, revenue
- **Skills:** Skill-based targeting
- **Groups:** Member interests
- **Education:** Degrees, schools

## Bidding Strategies

| Strategy | Best For |
|----------|----------|
| Automated (Maximize Delivery) | Awareness, lead gen |
| Maximum Cost Per Send (Message Ads) | Cost control on messaging |
| Cost Cap | Strict budget control |
| Bid Cap | Predictable costs |

## Quick Audit Checklist

- [ ] Review audience size (not too narrow/broad)
- [ ] Check engagement rate by creative
- [ ] Analyze CTR by format
- [ ] Review cost per send (message ads)
- [ ] Check form completion rate
- [ ] Verify targeting is B2B appropriate
- [ ] Test headline variations
- [ ] Review creative by job function/seniority
- [ ] Check for audience fatigue (frequency)

## B2B Specific Considerations

### Buying Cycle Alignment
| Stage | Objective | Format |
|-------|-----------|--------|
| Awareness | Brand visibility | Sponsored content, video |
| Consideration | Education | Carousel, document ads |
| Decision | Conversion | Lead gen forms, website visits |
| Retention | Engagement | Message ads, retargeting |

### Content That Works on LinkedIn
- Industry insights and statistics
- How-to and educational content
- Customer success stories
- Thought leadership
- Data visualizations
- Professional development content

### Content That Underperforms
- Overly promotional messaging
- B2C-style urgency tactics
- Generic stock imagery
- Clickbait headlines
- Low-quality design

## Common Issues & Solutions

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Low engagement | More educational content, test visuals |
| High cost per lead | Refine targeting, test lead forms |
| Low open rate (messages) | Test subject lines, refresh audience |
| High unsubscribe | Reduce frequency, improve content relevance |
| Not delivering | Expand targeting, increase bid |
| High CPC | Improve creative quality, refine audience |
