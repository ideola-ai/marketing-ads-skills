## Red Flag Thresholds (Universal)

| Metric | Red Flag | Critical | Immediate Action |
|--------|----------|----------|------------------|
| CTR vs Benchmark | -30% | -50% | Refresh creative |
| CPA vs Target | +50% | +100% | Check bid/budget |
| Frequency (weekly) | 4+ | 6+ | Pause/ad fatigue |
| ROAS | <1.5x | <1.0x | Critical - losing money |
| Quality Score | 4/10 | 1-3/10 | Fix assets/landing |
| Delivery (underspend) | <80% | <50% | Check budget/bids |
| Landing Page Load | >3s | >5s | Optimize immediately |
| Zero conversions | >3x Target CPA | >1x Target CPA with 0 clicks | KILL ad |
