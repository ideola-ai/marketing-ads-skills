# Mobile App Social Media Content Brief

## Executive Summary

Brief ini dirancang untuk mobile app B2C yang menargetkan Gen Z Indonesia (13-24 tahun) dengan tujuan **Brand Awareness**. Fokus pada konten Instagram yang relatable, authentic, dan menggunakan bahasa casual/friendly untuk membangun connection dengan audience yang sangat visual dan trend-driven.

## Target Audience Profile

### Primary Audience: Gen Z Indonesia (13-24 tahun)

**Demographics:**
- **Age Range:** 13-24 tahun (split: 13-17 teens, 18-24 young adults)
- **Gender:** All gender
- **Location:** Urban & suburban areas di Indonesia (Jakarta, Bandung, Surabaya, Medan, Yogyakarta, dll)
- **Income:** Students to early career, allowance-dependent to first income
- **Education:** SMA/K hingga early college/early career

**Psychographics:**
- **Interests:** Social media, gaming, K-pop/drama Korea, skincare, lifestyle, tech, memes, self-improvement
- **Values:** Authenticity, inclusivity, mental health awareness, sustainability, self-expression
- **Lifestyle:** Highly online, FOMO-driven, peer-influenced, mobile-first everything
- **Content Consumption:** Short-form video (Reels), relatable memes, aesthetic posts, behind-the-scenes

**Pain Points:**
- Bosan dengan konten brand yang "terlalu jualan"
- Sulit menemukan app yang benar-benar useful dan ngerti mereka
- Rasa FOMO terhadap tren terbaru
- Need for instant gratification dan easy solutions
- Low attention span (8 seconds max)

**Social Media Behavior:**
- **Peak Activity:** 7-9 AM (sebelum sekolah/kuliah), 12-2 PM (istirahat), 7-11 PM (after hours)
- **Content Preferences:** Relatable, funny, aesthetic, valuable, shareable
- **Engagement Style:** Reply dengan slang, save untuk later, share ke close friends
- **Platform Native:** Sangat paham Instagram features (Reels, Stories, Collabs, Guides)

**Key Motivations:**
- Ingin jadi bagian dari "tren" atau community
- Mencari validation dari peers
- Ingin hidup lebih "aesthetic" dan productive
- FOMO - takut ketinggalan sesuatu yang hype

## Platform Strategy: Instagram

### Content Priorities

**1. Instagram Reels (Highest Priority)**
- **Duration:** 7-15 seconds (sweet spot untuk Gen Z)
- **Style:** Fast-paced, trending sounds, hook di 1-2 detik pertama
- **Type:** POV transitions, tutorial singkat, relatable moments, aesthetic transitions

**2. Instagram Stories (Daily Engagement)**
- **Frequency:** 3-5 stories per hari
- **Content:** Behind the scenes, polls, Q&A, countdowns, casual updates
- **Features:** Interactive stickers, question box, slider, quiz

**3. Instagram Carousels (Save-able Content)**
- **Slides:** 5-10 slides per carousel
- **Purpose:** Educational content, tips, listicles, before-after
- **Goal:** Trigger saves dan shares

**4. Static Posts (Aesthetic Feed)**
- **Style:** Cohesive feed, minimal text on image, clean aesthetic
- **Caption:** Longer storytelling, engage dengan questions

## Visual Direction

### Overall Style

**Vibe:** Clean, modern, Gen Z aesthetic dengan playful touches

**Keywords:** Fresh, relatable, authentic, slightly eclectic but cohesive

### Color Palette

**Recommended Approaches (choose based on brand):**

**Option A: Minimalist Chic**
- Primary: Clean white, soft black
- Accent: One bold color (electric blue, lime green, coral)
- Vibe: Sophisticated but youthful

**Option B: Playful Pop**
- Primary: Black/white base
- Accent: 2-3 bright, saturated colors
- Vibe: Fun, energetic, bold

**Option C: Soft Aesthetic**
- Primary: Cream/beige, soft gray
- Accent: Pastel accents (sage green, lavender, peach)
- Vibe: Chill, approachable, dreamy

### Typography

**Headings:** Bold, modern sans-serif (Inter, Poppins, Space Grotesk)
**Body:** Clean, readable (works well on mobile)
**Accent:** Can use decorative font sparingly for emphasis

### Content Format Guidelines

**Reels:**
- Fast cuts (0.5-1 second per clip)
- Text overlay: Bold, readable, minimal
- Trending sounds yang relevan dengan brand
- Hook in first 1-2 seconds

**Carousels:**
- Consistent slide design (template with variations)
- Text: 1-2 key points per slide max
- Visual: Clean, not cluttered
- CTA on last slide

**Static Posts:**
- High-quality images or clean graphics
- Minimal text on image
- Cohesive with feed aesthetic
- Leave room for caption engagement

**Stories:**
- More casual, raw aesthetic OK
- Interactive elements (stickers, polls)
- Lower production value acceptable
- Frequent, daily content

### Mandatory Brand Elements

- **App Icon/Logo:** Visible but not overpowering
- **Brand Colors:** Consistent use across all content
- **Handle:** Include @handle on key visuals
- **Call to Action:** Clear but not pushy
